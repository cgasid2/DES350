Death Valley is the hottest,
driest and lowest national park.

It is a land of extremes yet
each has its own contrast.

From peaks frosted with winter
snow to rare rainstorms
bringing fields of wildflowers,
from tiny fish to refuge
for wildlife and humans.

Death Valley also
has many ghost towns, one
of them being the
Rhyolite, known as the
Queen City. Death Valley
is home to a great
diversity of wildlife.
